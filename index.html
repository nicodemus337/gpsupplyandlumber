About Us

Since 1937, G&P Supply & Lumber has supported the people, industries, and land of Acadiana. What began as a small, family-run hardware yard has grown into a trusted supplier for generations.

Industrial Supply Roots:
With over 40 years of industrial experience, we continue supplying fasteners, fittings, and hardware to salt mines, sugar mills, boat builders, farmers, mechanics, and fabrication yards.
