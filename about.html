Meet the owners

GP Supply & Lumber is locally owned and operated—built around real inventory and real service. Tell us the job. We’ll point you to what works.

Shannon Gonsoulin

Role: Store operations
Known for: Steady help, clear answers, and keeping the hometown yard strong.

Shannon has been walking into this store since the late ’70s with his father and grandfather. When something needed fixing, this was the stop. He cares about keeping business local, honoring the history of this area, and maintaining the level of service this store has been known for nearly 90 years.

Dan Price

Role: Store operations
Known for: Finding hard-to-track items and getting customers exactly what they need.

Dan is Loreauville-grown and came back home to his roots. He handles sales, specialty ordering, and inventory flow day to day. With a lifetime network of contacts, he can usually source what others can’t. His customers are his friends—and his reputation is built on follow-through, honesty, and reliability.
