Meet the owners

GP Supply & Lumber is locally owned and operated. The goal is simple: keep Loreauville supplied with real inventory, clear categories, and help that points you to what works.

Shannon Gonsoulin

Role: Store operations
Known for: Service

Shannon has been coming to this store since he was a little boy in the late 1970s—alongside his father and grandfather. When they needed something, they came here. That habit never stopped. GP has been his supply and hardware stop for most of his life, and today he’s committed to keeping that same reliability and service alive for the next generation.

He cares about keeping business in his hometown, honoring the history behind the store, and continuing the service that’s been provided on this corner for nearly 90 years. Customers should know this: we are highly service-oriented. Tell us what you’re trying to do, and we’ll help you leave with what works.
